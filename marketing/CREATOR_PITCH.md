## Slide 2 — Why Top Nest exists

Destiny's endgame is better with people you trust — but finding them is fragmented.

Top Nest brings together:

- **Verified raid & dungeon clears** (Bungie-linked, not self-reported)
- **Seasonal leaderboards** with real rewards

## Slide 5 — How scoring works (simple version)

- Points for **verified full completions** only  
- **2 pts** per clan member · **5 pts** per Random Fireteam Member found through Top Nest's fireteam finder  
- Raids: max 2 randos · Dungeons: max 1  
- **MVP votes:** +1 for voting, +3 for the Guardian you pick  
- Pantheon: each boss encounter counts as one raid worth of points  

Fair play enforced — suspicious runs held for review.
